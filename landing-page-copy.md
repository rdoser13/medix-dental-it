## DESIGN NOTES FOR IMPLEMENTATION

### Brand Colors (from style guide)
- **Primary:** Cool Gray 10 #63666A, Brand Gold #F2A900, Deep Purple #4B3048
- **Secondary:** Black #000000, Teal #007681, Light Blue #B9D9EB
- **CTA Buttons:** Brand Gold #F2A900 with white text (high contrast, action-oriented)
- **Section backgrounds:** Alternate white and light gray (#F5F5F5) for visual separation

### Typography
- H1: Montserrat Medium, 36–42px
- H2: Montserrat Regular, 28–32px
- H3: Montserrat Light, 22–24px
- Body: Lato Regular, 16–18px
- CTA Button: Montserrat Medium, 16px uppercase

### Keyword Placement Strategy (for Quality Score)
- **H1** contains "Dental IT Support Services" (exact match keyword)
- **H2s** contain "Dental Managed IT Services" and "Dental IT Support" variations
- **Body copy** naturally includes "dental IT," "dental IT support services," and "dental managed IT services" throughout
- **Form headline** reinforces "Dental IT"
- **Meta title** includes primary keyword
- This alignment between ad copy keywords → landing page H1/H2/body will boost Quality Score

### What Was Removed (and Why)
- **Team bios section** — Takes up too much space. Tom is mentioned as CEO in the differentiators. Full bios belong on an About page, not a conversion-focused landing page.
- **Project gallery** — Pretty photos but zero conversion value. Doesn't help someone decide to fill out the form.
- **Oversized "As Featured In" section** — Condensed to a small single-row logo strip. Still builds credibility without eating prime real estate.

### What Was Added (and Why)
- **Problem agitation section** — The searcher is already experiencing a pain point. Reflect it back to them before offering the solution. This is standard high-converting landing page structure.
- **Services grid** — People searching "dental managed IT services" need to see what's actually included. The current page never lists specific services.
- **How It Works** — Reduces friction. Shows the next step is easy and low-commitment.
- **Number of Locations dropdown** — Qualifies leads (single practice vs. DSO) and helps the sales team prioritize.
- **Biggest IT Challenge dropdown** — Pre-qualifies the lead AND gives the sales team a warm conversation opener.
- **Social proof near the hero** — One testimonial placed right next to the form. Don't make them scroll to find proof.
- **Stronger, benefit-driven headline** — "Keep Your Practice Running" speaks to the outcome, not just the service category.
- **Clear, repeated CTA** — Form at top, button at bottom. Two chances to convert.
